Register below for a secure investment with
Defence Housing Australia

When you invest in a Defence Housing Australia property, you get a guaranteed rental income for up to 12 years1, and because DHA is backed by the Australian Government, it's secure.

All of our investment properties undergo an annual rent review, and your rental income won't fall below the starting figure, even if the property market does2.
But the best thing is that we'll look after the day-to-day aspects of your investment—pay your rent, manage your tenants and take care of repairs and maintenance.

We'll even spruce up the property at the end of the lease3.

With DHA you can look forward to tomorrow... and today.
